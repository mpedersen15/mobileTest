On the Weekend

When I'm planning a vacation...

I want to relax! Sitting poolside with a pina colada sounds like the ideal break to me
I want to be a tourist! No lounging for me - I want to see all the sights!

On a night that I don't feel like cooking, and want to eat out...

I make a beeline for the local burger joint and gorge myself on some delicious comfort fast food
I make reservations at the classy, chic new bistro/wine bar and head downtown dressed to the nines

If I have a few free hours, and a good book in my hand...

I'm going to put it back on the shelf. Television was invented for a reason, you know
I'll grab a few more to add to it and get comfortable - I won't be moving for while
